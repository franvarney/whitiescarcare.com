About

Whities Car Care is an auto repair shop specializing in servicing and preventative maintenance for Asian automobiles located on 1701 Madison Road in Cincinnati. For over 70 years, we've serviced vehicles within the Cincinnati community. We employ a professional and trustworthy staff that will go the extra mile to make sure you're satisfied. We're a small business that focuses on the quality of the work we do, not the quantity. We've proven ourselves, winning the best auto repair award from City Beat ten years in a row. Call us to schedule an appointment during our business hours: 7:30am to 6:00pm, Monday through Friday.
